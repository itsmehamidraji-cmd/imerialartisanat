Our Craft

Woven from the
soul of Morocco

Every thread, every knot, every carved leg carries the memory of Marrakech. Our artisans have inherited their craft through generations — no machines, no shortcuts.

  • 🧵
    Berber Wool — hand-spun, naturally dyed from mountain plants & minerals
  • 🌲
    Atlas Cedar — aromatic, insect-resistant, ages beautifully over decades
  • 🌾
    Natural Rattan — ethically sourced, woven in traditional Marrakech patterns
  • 🪨
    Recycled Textiles — Boucherouite poufs made from upcycled fabric scraps
